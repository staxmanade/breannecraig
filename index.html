About the artist

Hi, I’m Breanne.

I’m a commission-based watercolor artist in Reno, Nevada, and a lifelong animal lover. I paint every portrait by hand, paying close attention to the bright eyes, crooked ears, soft expressions, and little quirks that make your loved one unmistakably them.

My goal is simple: to create a piece that feels personal, joyful, and worthy of a place in your home.
